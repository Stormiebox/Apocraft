## Apocraft
Welcome to Apocraft, a vanilla-friendly crafting overhaul for Project Zomboid! Explore over 1,000 diverse crafting recipes, paired with unique SFX for a heightened experience. Tailored for compatibility with other mods, and powered by "Crafting Enhanced Core", Apocraft is set to redefine your survival journey!

## Guide
Crafting Categories
- Cooking: Dive into gourmet survival with an expansive array of recipes.
- Carpentry: Enhance your building and woodworking skills with advanced blueprints.
- Electrical: Power up your survival with innovative electrical setups.
- Farming: Go green with extended farming capabilities.
- Forging (Smithing): Work with metals like never before, forge tools, jewelry and more.
- Medical: Broaden your first-aid crafting repertoire for those dire moments.
- Metalworking: Dive deeper into metalwork, crafting everything from tools to intricate designs.
- Tailoring: Stitch, repair, and craft outfits to survive in style.
- Survivalist: Essential crafts to aid your longevity in the apocalypse.
- Lumberjack: Master the art of woodworking and logging, leveraging both strength and skill to harvest more resources and craft intricate woodworks. <!-- New Category -->

Enhanced Crafting Features
- Diverse Paint Crafting: Bring color to the apocalypse! Craft different paint shades using real-life mixing techniques or mix existing in-game paint and hair dye colors.
- Canning Galore: Preserve your food and resources. Now you can can a variety of foods for extended shelf life.
- Hair Dye Crafting: Revamp your look by crafting a range of hair dye colors, using methods similar to paint crafting.
- Extensive Recycling: Give a second life to your items. Recycle a vast majority of in-game items into valuable resources like scrap metal, ripped sheets, denim strips, and more.
- Weapon/Tool Repair: Don't throw away your favorite tools! Repair weapons and tools that weren't repairable in the base game.
- Glass Molding: A fresh introduction to the game. Craft glass, shape them for vehicle windows or other utility, and innovate in survival.

Additional Features
- Unique SFX for Crafting: Each crafting category gets its own auditory flair, elevating your crafting experience.
- Custom-made Magazine Textures: Discover and learn new recipes through exquisitely designed magazine items.
- Molds for Enhanced Forging: Use custom molds for various purposes, including creating glass panes, iron ingots, or even melting down jewelry.

Vanilla-Friendly & Compatibility
- Vanilla Synergy: Designed with the base game in mind, Apocraft integrates seamlessly into your vanilla Project Zomboid experience, enhancing it without overhauling the core essence.
- Mod Compatibility: Apocraft is meticulously crafted to ensure compatibility with a majority of mods available in the Project Zomboid workshop. Dive in with confidence!

Crafting Tables & Required Mod
- New Crafting Tables: Apocraft introduces specialized crafting tables essential for the new recipes. These tables bring structure and a unique touch to the crafting experience.
- Crafting Enhanced Core: For the crafting tables to function, Crafting Enhanced Core is a mandatory mod. This core mod powers the new crafting tables, creating a symbiotic relationship for the best experience.

## FAQs
- **Q: Can I use this mod alongside XYZ mod?**
  - A: Apocraft is designed for compatibility with most Project Zomboid mods. However, always refer to the mod compatibility list or test in a safe environment first.

- **Q: How do I repair my tools?**
  - A: With Apocraft, tool repairing becomes intuitive. Simply gather the required materials as indicated in the crafting menu and proceed as you would with any other crafting process.

- **Q: I see a new skill called "Lumberjack". How does it work?**
  - A: As you use axes and partake in woodworking activities, your Lumberjack skill will improve. As you level up, you'll notice increased efficiency in logging, granting bonus wood and resources.  <!-- New FAQ -->
